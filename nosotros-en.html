Foodify

A bit of history

The idea of creating Foodify was born in 2020 during a family meal in the small kitchen of our founders, Marta and Andrés. Both passionate about gastronomy and with an inexhaustible entrepreneurial spirit, they began dreaming of the possibility of taking fast food to a new level: quality and speed accessible to everyone, in the comfort of their homes.

With humble beginnings but a clear vision, Marta and Andrés decided to join forces to create a business that not only offered delicious dishes but also ensured fast and efficient service. Thus, in 2021, they opened their first kitchen in a small venue in the heart of Etxebarri, with a carefully crafted menu that combined the best of traditional and modern cuisine.

Thanks to the dedication and effort of the team, the company quickly gained popularity. Orders increased, and the satisfaction of our customers became our greatest ally. Within a few months, Foodify became the benchmark for home-delivered fast food in the region.

In 2023, with success firmly established, we expanded our operations and opened several branches in different cities. Today, Foodify has a committed and passionate team, always ready to exceed our customers' expectations, providing a unique and memorable culinary experience.

At Foodify, we believe that good food can brighten anyone's day. That's why every dish we prepare is made not only with the best ingredients but also with the love and dedication that define us. Thank you for allowing us to be part of your special moments!
